Carbonara

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add your pasta. Cook until al dente.
  2. Cook the bacon: In a large skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pan.
  3. Make the sauce: In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Combine the ingredients: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the bacon fat and toss to coat. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the egg mixture, stirring quickly to coat the pasta evenly. The residual heat from the pasta and skillet will cook the egg and create a creamy sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of pasta water to thin it out.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Crumble the cooked bacon over the pasta and sprinkle with additional grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
