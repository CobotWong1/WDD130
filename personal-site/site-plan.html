Content

Home page

The home page will introduce myself as a chef, where is my kitchen and what prize I have got. I will have my personal photo to let everyone who will meet me.

The people will come to my site want to learn to cook, or see what dishes I can make, so the home page to introduce myself can let people believe that my skills and experience in cooking are available. So the main purpose is to let people know who am I.

Second, for the people who come to my site and want to learn cooking, I will have another child page that contains my own recipes, but I will have a dish photo to let people know there has a link to have more recipes. Another child page will be Food Photography which let others submit their dishes photos.

So my audiences can expect what they can find on my site.

Introduction example is :

I am Cobot Wong, I have over 20 years of being a chef in hotels, and I have got 2 prizes for the most valuable dishes. Now I open my own restaurant and I will share my dishes with everyone.

Restaurant address example is : XXXX city, XXXX road, XXXXshop opening and closing time: 12:00 - 21:00

Recipe Page

This child page is the recipe page, the people who want to learn to cook new dishes will come here to this site. As there are some steps to follow and some ingredient quantities, of course, I will have a finished dish photo as an example to let them see, also down below will have some tips for cooking this dish.

after this dish recipe, down below and above than footer will have other recipes to let them continue searching for, there will have the photo and the dish's name will transparent in photo, also link will provide when click the image.

Images for the Page 2

Food Photography

This child page is Food Photography, the people who want to see others' products or want to share their own products after they follow the recipe. Here is the site who can explore their interest in cooking.

It is Food Photography so there will be fewer paragraphs and more photos, and will have the creator's name down below the photo. I will use gridlines here to have around 5*5 products there.
